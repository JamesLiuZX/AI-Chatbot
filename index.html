Dunman Helper

A simple and elegant chatbot for anyone interested in finding out more about Dunman High.

Learn More

Dunman Helper is a chatbot based on api.ai,with both text and speech functions,taking data from data.gov.sg and Dunman High School web to provide interested individuals with more details of the school, including its courses, students, contacts, and news.
